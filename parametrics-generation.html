PARAMETRICS & GENERATION

ABOUT

Taking a simple letter from the alphabet like the letter C, I used P5 to create sketches that explored new and interesting ways of designing. These daily sketches helped me to experiment and understand the tools used in coding and push the limits when it came to generative art making. Using previous techniques, I learned to implement them into new sketches daily, which eventually helped me get a better grasp of P5.
